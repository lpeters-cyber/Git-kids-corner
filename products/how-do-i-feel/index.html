How do I Feel?

Pretend Play

R140

How do I feel? is an emotion-packed game that gets kids talking about their feelings in a fun and engaging way! This innovative game features two sets of 4 foam cubes – yellow cubes for happy, excited, and proud and red cubes for sad, angry, and afraid. Kids will learn emotional intelligence as they take turns acting out the emotion cubes with hilarious and exaggerated expressions.Features and benefits:– Recognize and express positive and negative emotions– Develop empathy and emotional skills– Hilarious, interactive play for 1-2 Children– For ages 4+ yearsWith How do I feel? your child will gain the tools to understand their own emotions while having fun making silly faces with you. It’s the perfect way to start healthy conversations about mental health.
