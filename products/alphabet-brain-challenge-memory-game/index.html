Alphabet Brain Challenge memory game

Learning Tools

R140

This fun and education game improves memory and observation skills as well as vocabulary and knowledge of the alphabet (initial sounds).How to play (2 or more players).Place the cards picture side up in a pile. Draw a card and turn the timer over. Study the card until the timer runs out. Pass the card to another player and roll the dice. The other player will turn the card over and ask the appropriate question. For example, if a 2 is rolled, you must answer quetion 2 on the back of the card. The other player reads the question and if answered correctly, that card is kept by the player. If not, it is returned to the bottom of the pile. The player with the most cards wins!
