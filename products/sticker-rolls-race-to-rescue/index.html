Sticker Rolls - Race to Rescue

Sticker Pads

R175

These stickers are packed with action . . . and creative possibilities! Four themed sticker rolls come loaded on a sturdy wooden dispenser for hassle-free tabletop storage. Just pull out what you need, then tear off along the perforation. There are more than 75 feet (23.8m) of stickers in all! Each sticker sheet includes one main figure--such as a pitcher, firefighter, airplane or knight--along with gear to get him ready for his next adventure. Includes seven vehicles, six people at work, six athletes and six medieval figures (x13 of each design). Kids can use them together to make exciting scenes, decorate notebooks, pencil cases and more, or tear and share with a friend.
